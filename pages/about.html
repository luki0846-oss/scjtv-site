ABOUT SCJ TV

SCJ is an abbreviation for Shincheonji, and SCJ TV is the official broadcasting channel of the Shincheonji Church of Jesus, the Temple of the Tabernacle of the Testimony.

SCJ TV is a global broadcast channel that brings together worship, the Word, education, and Heavenly Culture. Across regions and languages, we aim to provide audiences worldwide with access to the same revealed Word and Heavenly Culture—wherever they are.

OUR VISION

Building a Global Broadcasting Network for the Revealed Word

SCJ TV is committed to building a stable global broadcasting network that can deliver the gospel of Revelation— grounded in the love of God and Jesus Christ—without interruption, anywhere in the world.

Through the promised revealed Word in Jesus Christ, and through worship and Heavenly Culture that reflect God’s will, SCJ TV seeks to deliver content with accuracy and consistency beyond differences in region, language, and environment— so that audiences worldwide can experience the Word and broadcasting by the same standard.

By broadcasting the gospel of Revelation, SCJ TV works to ensure that the love of God and the teachings of Jesus are shared across the world, while continuing to develop a reliable and sustainable global broadcasting system.
